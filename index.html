Welcome to the Protected Paws NFT collection.
The 1st ever NFT collection which is created by rescued Dogs. The Dogs have used the peanut butter painting technique and AI to create 6999 unique NFTs.
It all started when Ujjwal (currently in 7th grade) and his dogs started using the peanut butter painting technique to create various art in his house. He realised that his dogs Happy and Joy loved creating these paintings. That’s when he got the idea of selling these as NFTs.
J69 group was more than happy to take up Protected Paws as their 1st NFT project. J69 decided that they would approach the Samarpan Foundation in Bangalore with this idea to create more such art pieces and spend time with the rescued Dogs. The rest is history.
To know how the funds are being utilised to better develop the rescue centre please follow our Instagram and Twitter handle.,
